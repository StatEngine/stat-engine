Funding

Funding for StatEngine has been awarded to Prominent Edge under the financial assistance award 70NANB17H161 from U.S. Department of Commerce, National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) and the Public Safety Innovation Accelerator Program (PSIAP) . This program funds forward-looking, innovative R&D focused on solving the novel challenges posed by the criticality and complexity of the data relevant to the public safety operational environment. The goal of this effort is to support the creation of an open source analytics and visualization platform that can be costÂ­ effectively leveraged by Public Safety Organizations of any size to exploit large volumes of complex, highly relevant and impactful data sets in real time. The period of performance for StatEngine is from May 2017 through May 2019.
